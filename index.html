Meet Your Guide

Hi, I'm Coco Love

Spiritual Guide · Poet · Creator

I am a poet, storyteller and motivational speaker from Dallas, Texas. I believe in the power of alchemy, and I use my writing as a way to heal, and transform pain to beauty and a deeper understanding of life. My poetry is a channeling from Spirit and often, a love letter to the parts of ourselves we're taught to hide.
Additionally, the jewelry I create is an extension of the same intention with each piece handcrafted to carry energy, tell a story, and to remind you of your own worth.

"Whether you find me through a piece of jewelry, a spiritual reading, or a poem — I want you to leave feeling a little lighter, a little more rooted, and a whole lot more you."
